About

Who

Anyone who aspires to be better than they were the day before. It doesn’t matter what your age, race, size or shape is, you can start your fitness journey from your living room with this workout guide.

What

A workout designed to build muscle and burn fat in any environment. The only thing needed to succeed with this guide is hard work and motivation to be better. It doesn’t matter what your fitness level is, this workout can beat you up even in quarantine.

Where

Your living room, driveway, bedroom…anywhere you want! As long as you have the drive and ambition to achieve your fitness goals, these workouts can be done just about anywhere.

When

Whenever you feel like it. As long as you’re completing these workouts at least four times a week, it doesn’t matter what time you do them. Although any time is good, it is best to complete them in the morning or at night.

Why

Staying active has always been an important part of your health. With everything that’s been going on we need to remember that exercise relieves stress, lowers anxiety, keeps the mind steady and so much more.
